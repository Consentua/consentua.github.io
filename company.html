About Consentua

What is Consentua?

Consentua is a flexible consent management system that helps organisations to build stronger and safer customer relationships.

Consentua is simple enough to deploy alongside your existing enterprise systems and to adopt incrementally, flexible enough to fit seamlessly with your brand, and powerful enough to cope with whatever requirements you need to throw at it.
