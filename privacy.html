Privacy policy

What this notice covers

This privacy notice tells you what to expect when KnowNow Information Ltd. & associated brands (for example Consentua) collects personal information. It explains how we use your personal data and your rights in relation to that data.

The information collected by KnowNow Information

KnowNow Information collects information about you from a variety of sources, including:

  • Information we collect from you directly
  • Information we collect about you when you call us, visit our sites, use our mobile applications or services, or view our online advertisements.
  • Information we collect about you from other sources, such as social media, public and commercially available sources.

All the information KnowNow Information collects about you may be combined to improve our communications with you, and to develop world-class products and services.

Uses & information sharing

KnowNow Information uses the information we collect for KnowNow Information business purposes such as:

  • To provide the services you request.
  • To tell you about KnowNow Information services and those offered by our carefully selected business partners.
  • To manage our websites and services that we provide.
  • We do not sell your personal information to marketing companies.
  • We keep your data within the European Union (EU) or countries with EU adequacy status.

We may share your information:

  • With our suppliers for the provision of professional services, but only if the business client has granted consent.
  • With our third-party service providers who perform KnowNow Information business operations on our behalf, with your consent.
  • As part of a sale of a KnowNow Information brand to another company.
  • To protect and defend KnowNow Information (including enforcing our terms and conditions).
  • When required by law and/or government authorities.
  • You have the right not to provide personal information to KnowNow Information. This may mean KnowNow Information is not able to provide the services you request or to tell you about other services offered by KnowNow Information.

Your choices:

  • You can stop receiving promotional email or postal communications from KnowNow Information by following the instructions in those messages or through this privacy notice.
  • You have the right to withdraw your consent for the processing of your personal data by KnowNow Information.
  • You have the right to have your personal data erased (right to be forgotten), KnowNow Information will make every reasonable effort to remove any reference to your personal data if they have shared it with any 3rd party in the course of their usual business processes.
  • You have the right to have your personal data transferred to another company and KnowNow Information will make every effort to comply with your request.
  • You have the right to raise any concerns to the processing or use of your personal data by KnowNow Information to an appropriate data protection authority.
  • You have the right to review personal data KnowNow Information hold on you if this does not impact on other individuals rights and freedoms.

KnowNow Information sites may include links to third-party sites. KnowNow Information does not control these third-party sites, and we encourage you to read the privacy policy of every site you visit.

KnowNow Information sites may also have “plugins” (such as the Facebook “Like” button) to third-party sites or offer login (such as login with Facebook) through a third-party account. Third-party plugins and login features, including their loading, operation and use, are governed by the privacy policy and terms of the third-party providing them.

Important information

KnowNow Information is committed to working with clients and data protection authorities to resolve any privacy concern.

KnowNow Information complies with EU data protection laws.

Contact

If you have any questions about any element of our privacy policy, or if you would like to contact our data administrator, please e-mail contact@consentua.com.

Privacy updates

This notice is reviewed by KnowNow Information on a regular basis and should be checked by individuals for the latest updates.

Latest updated: 10/07/2018

KnowNow Information sites may include links to third-party sites. KnowNow Information does not control these third-party sites, and we encourage you to read the privacy policy of every site you visit.

Our websites may include plugins from social networks or other third parties.

The social network or third party may also automatically collect information such as your IP address, information about your browser and device, and the address of the web page you are visiting on our site. The login feature may also place and read cookies from that third party that may contain a unique identifier the social network or other third party assigns to you. The functionality of and your use of the login is governed by the privacy policy and terms of the party that provided the login functionality.
